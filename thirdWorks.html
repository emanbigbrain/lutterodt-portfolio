OnSpeed

Context

OnSpeed was a very early concept of a start up company based in Jonkoping that provides same day delivery solutions. The business plan was developed alongside the Business Planning and Entrepreneurship course at the School of Engineering, Jonkoping University. The UI/UX of this same day delivery app, is supposed to enhance the user's experience with simple and intuitive taps. The UI is specifically tailored for the target audience, and has been iterated multiple times to adhere to common UX and Design principles.

Duration

3.5 Weeks
